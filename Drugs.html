Vlasenko Maksym
The impact of drugs on the body
Drugs have a devastating impact on the human body. They affect the brain, causing addiction and disrupting normal bodily functions. Long-term drug use can lead to serious illnesses such as heart disease, liver disease, kidney disease, as well as mental disorders, including depression, anxiety, and psychosis. Drugs can lead to death, both as a result of overdose and due to related health complications.
The harm of tobacco smoking
Tobacco smoking is one of the most common harmful habits in the world. Tobacco smoke contains thousands of chemicals, many of which are carcinogens. Smoking is the leading cause of lung cancer, and also increases the risk of developing other types of cancer, cardiovascular disease, respiratory diseases, and many other health problems. Nicotine, found in tobacco, is highly addictive.
How to avoid drug and tobacco use
Avoiding drug and tobacco use is possible if you adhere to a healthy lifestyle, have strong social connections, and are aware of the risks associated with these substances. It is important to develop the skills to refuse offers, be confident in yourself, and choose friends who do not use drugs or tobacco. If you or someone you know has problems with drugs or tobacco, it is important to seek professional help.
